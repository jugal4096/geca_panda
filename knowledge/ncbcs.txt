GECA CBCS RULES 
Government College of Engineering Aurangabad 

Rules and Regulations regarding Curriculum as per Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) 
for Undergraduate B.Tech Programmes. 

Academic year: 2021-2022 onwards 

-------------------------------------------------- 

INTRODUCTION 

These regulations apply to all undergraduate degree programs at 
Government College of Engineering Aurangabad (GECA). 

The rules define the policies for: 

- admission of students 
- academic curriculum 
- examinations and evaluations 
- certification of degrees 

These rules may be revised by the Academic Council when required. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

ACADEMIC CALENDAR 

The B.Tech degree duration: 

Full Time Programme → 8 semesters 
Part Time Programme → 8 semesters 

Each academic year has two semesters. 

Each semester normally contains: 
- academic teaching 
- examinations 
- semester vacation 

The academic calendar defines all important dates. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

REGISTRATION 

Students must register for courses at the beginning of each semester. 

Minimum credits per semester: 
Full time programme → 10 credits 
Part time programme → 10 credits 

Maximum credits: 
Full time programme → 30 credits 
Part time programme → 30 credits 

Students must complete registration before the deadline. 

Late registration may be allowed with approval. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

CREDIT AND EVALUATION SYSTEM 

Academic performance is graded on a 10 point scale. 

Grades and grade points: 

A+ → 10 
A  → 9 
B+ → 8 
B  → 7 
C+ → 6 
C  → 5 
D  → 4 
F  → Fail 

SGPA = Semester Grade Point Average 

CGPA = Cumulative Grade Point Average 

Division based on CGPA: 

CGPA ≥ 7.0 → First Class with Distinction 
6.0 ≤ CGPA < 7.0 → First Class 
5.0 ≤ CGPA < 6.0 → Second Class 
CGPA < 5.0 → Pass Class 

-------------------------------------------------- 

ADD / DROP COURSES 

Students can ADD courses after registration if permitted. 

Students can DROP courses within the deadline 
specified in the academic calendar. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

ATTENDANCE RULES 

Students must attend lectures, tutorials and practical sessions. 

Minimum attendance required → 75% 

If attendance is below 75%, 
the student may not be allowed to appear for End Semester Examination. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

ABSENCE DURING SEMESTER 

Leave of absence may be granted for valid reasons such as: 

- medical emergencies 
- family emergencies 
- official representation of institute 

However, all In-Semester evaluations are mandatory. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

TRANSFER OF CREDITS 

Students may transfer credits earned from: 

- recognized institutes 
- approved external courses 

Credit transfer must be approved by the Academic Council. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

WITHDRAWAL FROM PROGRAMME 

Temporary withdrawal may be permitted for: 

- serious illness 
- family emergencies 
- other valid reasons 

Permanent withdrawal requires formal approval from the institute. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

CONDUCT AND DISCIPLINE 

Students must maintain proper conduct inside and outside campus. 

Prohibited activities include: 

- ragging 
- possession of alcohol or drugs 
- violence or misconduct 
- hacking or computer misuse 
- plagiarism 

Violations may result in disciplinary action. 

-------------------------------------------------- 

GRADUATION REQUIREMENTS 

A student is eligible for degree award if: 

- all required credits are completed 
- no dues remain 
- no disciplinary cases are pending 

-------------------------------------------------- 

DEGREE PROGRAMMES AT GECA 

The institute offers B.Tech programmes in: 

- Civil Engineering 
- Computer Science and Engineering 
- Electrical Engineering 
- Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering 
- Information Technology 
- Mechanical Engineering 

-------------------------------------------------- 

ADMISSION 

Admissions follow rules of Government of Maharashtra. 

Students may also participate in: 

- student exchange programs 
- credit transfer programs 

-------------------------------------------------- 

END OF CBCS RULES
